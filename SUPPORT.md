# Support

Need help with a Web Tech Domains project? Here's where to go.

## Documentation

Check the repository's `README.md` and `documentation/` folder (where present) first — most common questions are answered there.

## Reporting a Bug

Open a [bug report](https://github.com/Web-Tech-Domains/.github/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml) on the relevant repository.

## Requesting a Feature

Open a [feature request](https://github.com/Web-Tech-Domains/.github/issues/new?template=feature_request.yml).

## Asking a Question

Open a [question issue](https://github.com/Web-Tech-Domains/.github/issues/new?template=question.yml), or reach us directly:

- 📧 **General enquiries:** info@webtechdomains.in
- 🛟 **Support:** support@webtechdomains.in
- ☎️ **Phone:** +91-86170-31783
- 🕒 **Hours:** Monday – Saturday, 9 AM – 7 PM IST

## Security Issues

Do **not** open a public issue for security vulnerabilities. See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for private reporting instructions.
